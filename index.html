Welcome to SA Impeachment Bingo! This site lets you generate and use bingo cards for the SA president's Impeachment trials. This will also be live-streamed on Instagram @ualbanysenate and the proceedings are open to the public. If you have any ideas for squares on the card feel free to reach out to me on Discord @TenebrisLux#8353 in the SUNY Albany Discord Server

Update: You can now get back your old card layouts by using seeds. Copy the seed of your old card into the text field and hit the button. Each seed coresponds to a unique card, so the structure of one card's seed will be preserved.

Special thanks to reddit user u/hugcartel for designing the original SA Impeachment Bingo and inspiring this project. Special thanks to ManWithoutModem for volunteering to become the emperor of UAlbany In these dark times, May his reign be endless.
